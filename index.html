About Me

Howdy! My name is Hamzah Issa, an Electrical Engineering student at Texas A&M University. I was born and raised in Fort Worth, Texas, and then moved to several different countries with my family where I was exposed to a wide variety of cultures and ethnicities. My drive and passion for a sustainable and technologically advanced future led me to pursue a degree in electrical engineering. During my time at Texas A&M, I was able to build my leadership, communication and hands-on skills by joining several professional organizations such as HKN-IEEE where I led a team that worked on engineering projects and SHPE where I gained vital software knowledge on the technical division team. My latest experience is the Electric Formula SAE organization on campus where I am a member of the electronics team, and the IT specialist job position I got on campus.
